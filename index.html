Furtiluna YouTube channel

Welcome to the Furtiluna YouTube channel, a dedicated space for traders who want to develop their skills through education, structure, and innovation. This channel is led by the founder of Furtiluna and is designed to support traders at every stage of their journey, from beginners learning the fundamentals to experienced traders exploring automated and algorithmic trading solutions.

Our content focuses on practical and actionable insights that help you move your trading forward in a responsible and sustainable way. You will find demonstrations of algorithmic trading results generated using our custom-built Expert Advisors, along with clear explanations of how automated trading systems function in real-world conditions. In addition to automation, the channel covers broader trading education, including market behaviour, system-based decision-making, and mindset development. Whether your goal is to understand the basics of trading or to evaluate systematic and algorithm-driven approaches, our videos aim to provide structured knowledge without unrealistic promises or hype.

By subscribing to the Furtiluna channel, you gain access to educational tools and insights intended to help you navigate the financial markets with clarity and integrity. Our mission is to promote informed decision-making, realistic expectations, and long-term growth rather than short-term speculation.

FAQs

Clear answers to common questions traders ask before starting.

How much money do I need to start trading?

There isn’t one “correct” number. Start with an amount you can afford to lose while learning, and focus on risk management first. Many traders begin on a demo account to build process and consistency before using real capital.

Can I borrow money to trade?

Furtiluna does not recommend borrowing money to trade. Debt increases pressure and can lead to poor decisions. Only use risk capital you can afford to lose.

What is Forex?

Forex (foreign exchange) is the market where currencies are exchanged (e.g., EUR/USD). Prices move based on supply and demand, macroeconomic factors, and market positioning.

What is a trading robot / Expert Advisor (EA)?

A trading robot (often called an EA) is software that follows predefined rules to analyze markets and place/manage trades. Automation doesn’t remove risk — it only executes rules consistently.

Do you provide signals or “guaranteed” trades?

No. Furtiluna focuses on education, systems, and responsible decision-making — not promises, hype, or guaranteed outcomes.

Are results guaranteed?

No. Trading involves risk, and performance varies by market conditions, execution, psychology, and risk control. Past or simulated results don’t guarantee future results.

How much time do I need each week?

It depends on your goals. Most traders benefit from a consistent routine: learning, journaling, reviewing risk, and testing. The focus is quality and repeatability, not screen time.

Is this financial advice?

No. Furtiluna content is educational only and not financial, investment, or trading advice.
